Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Photo Royal collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app and related services.

Information we may access

With your permission, the app may access content on your device that is necessary to provide requested features, such as your photo library or videos for editing. We do not access these categories without your consent.

How information is used

We may use information to:

  • provide editing features you request;
  • maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the app;
  • respond to support requests and policy inquiries;
  • comply with legal obligations.

Advertising

The app may display advertising through third-party ad partners. These services may process limited technical information as required to deliver, measure, or protect ads.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers and partners only where reasonably necessary to operate the app, support users, comply with law, or protect our rights.

Data retention

Information is retained only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices

You can manage certain permissions through your device settings, including photo library access and notification preferences.

Children's privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age prohibited by applicable law without parental or guardian consent.

Contact
