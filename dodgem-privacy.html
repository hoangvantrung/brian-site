Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dodgem handles information when you use the game and related services.

Dodgem is an Android arcade game focused on short dodge-based gameplay sessions and may include in-app advertising provided by Google AdMob.

Gameplay and device data

Dodgem may process limited technical information from your device that is necessary to run gameplay, maintain stability, and support analytics or ad delivery.

Advertising

Dodgem may display advertising from third-party partners such as Google AdMob. These partners may process device identifiers, approximate location, ad performance data, or other limited technical data as needed to deliver, measure, and protect ads in accordance with their policies.

Personal information

Dodgem does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, address, or phone number unless you contact us directly for support.

How information is used

Information may be used to:

  • operate and improve the game;
  • measure performance, crashes, and gameplay stability;
  • serve and measure advertising;
  • respond to support and legal requests.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited data may be shared with service providers and advertising partners only as needed to operate the game, measure ads, comply with the law, or protect our rights.

Children's privacy

Dodgem is not intended for children under the age prohibited by applicable law without parental or guardian consent.

Your choices

You can manage ad personalization and certain permissions through your device settings where available.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Dodgem after an update means the revised policy applies from its effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact contact@naemomlab.com.
